Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last updated: January 31, 2025

Introduction

Greenville Run Clubs ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices for our mobile application and website. Simply put: we don't collect, store, or share your personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The Greenville Run Clubs app operates without requiring user accounts, email addresses, or any personal information. All app functionality works locally on your device.

No Data Storage

Since we don't collect any personal data, we don't store any information about you on our servers. Any preferences or settings you configure within the app are stored locally on your device only.

No Data Sharing

We cannot share what we don't collect. No personal information is transmitted from the app to us or to any third parties.

Website Analytics

Our website may use basic, anonymous analytics to understand general usage patterns (such as page views), but this does not include any personally identifiable information.

Contact Information

If you contact us directly via email for support, we will only use your email address to respond to your inquiry and will not store it for any other purpose or add you to any mailing lists.

Location Services

The app may request location permissions to show you nearby running clubs and events. This location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or stored by us.

Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, including children, there are no special privacy concerns for users under 13.

Third-Party Services

The app displays information about local running clubs and events. This information is publicly available and does not involve the collection of your personal data.

App Store

When you download our app from the App Store, Apple may collect certain information according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to this information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: greenvillerunclubs@markgingrass.com
